About Blessed Sunny Medical Company & De Blessed Sunny Trading Co.

We operate two complementary businesses serving Nigeria's healthcare sector. Blessed Sunny Medical Company specializes in the manufacturing of quality medical products, while De Blessed Sunny Trading Co. focuses on the distribution and supply of medical equipment and consumables.

Our dual expertise allows us to maintain complete quality control from production to delivery, ensuring that healthcare providers across Nigeria receive reliable, affordable, and certified medical solutions. Located in Kaduna, we serve hospitals, clinics, pharmacies, and healthcare institutions nationwide.

Our commitment to excellence, regulatory compliance, and customer satisfaction has positioned us as a dependable name in Nigeria's medical supply chain.

What We Offer

Medical Manufacturing

Blessed Sunny Medical Company produces a range of medical consumables and supplies using quality raw materials and adherence to strict manufacturing standards. Our production facility maintains hygiene protocols and quality assurance processes that meet regulatory requirements.

Medical Trading & Distribution

De Blessed Sunny Trading Co. sources and supplies medical equipment, devices, and consumables from certified manufacturers. We maintain adequate inventory levels to ensure prompt delivery and competitive pricing for our clients.

Bulk Supply Solutions

We provide bulk supply contracts for hospitals, government health facilities, and large healthcare institutions. Our logistics network ensures timely delivery across Nigeria.

Quality Assurance

Every product that leaves our facility undergoes rigorous quality control checks. We maintain proper documentation, certifications, and traceability for all our medical products.

Customer Support

Our experienced team provides technical support, product training, and after-sales service to ensure optimal use of our medical supplies and equipment.
